1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Prof Dr Mahmoud Selim Video Conferencing App ("App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Display Name: Optional name you enter when joining meetings (not stored permanently)
  • Meeting Codes: Meeting IDs you create or enter to join video calls (stored locally only)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Camera Access: Live video during calls (not recorded or stored by us)
  • Microphone Access: Live audio during calls (not recorded or stored by us)
  • Meeting History: List of meeting codes you've joined (stored locally on your device only)
  • Device Information: Basic device specs for app functionality

2.3 Information NOT Collected

  • ❌ We do NOT require account creation or registration
  • ❌ We do NOT collect email addresses or personal contact information
  • ❌ We do NOT record or store video/audio from your calls
  • ❌ We do NOT use analytics services or track your behavior
  • ❌ We do NOT collect location data

3. How We Use Your Information

All data collection serves the sole purpose of enabling video conferencing:

  • Video & Audio: Real-time transmission during active calls only
  • Meeting Codes: Remember meetings you've joined for quick rejoin
  • Display Name: Identify you to other participants during calls

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 WebRTC & Janus Gateway Server

Our app uses native WebRTC with Janus Gateway for video conferencing:

  • What They Do: Provide video/audio signaling and media routing infrastructure
  • Server: Janus Gateway server (wss://webrtc.merge.ws:8989)
  • What They Access: Video, audio, and meeting metadata during active calls
  • Privacy Policy: https://janus.conf.meetecho.com/
  • Encryption: WebRTC DTLS-SRTP encryption for all media streams

4.2 No Other Third Parties

  • We do NOT use Google Analytics, Firebase, or tracking services
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT sell or rent your information

5. Data Security

  • In-Transit Encryption: All video/audio is encrypted during transmission
  • End-to-End Encryption: WebRTC encryption for calls
  • Local Storage: Meeting history in app-private storage only
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data never leaves your device

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Data Control

  • Access: View your meeting history in the app
  • Deletion: Clear meeting history anytime through app settings
  • Permissions: Control camera/microphone in Android Settings
  • Uninstall: Delete the app to remove all data

6.2 Camera & Microphone Permissions

Required for Video Calls: Camera and microphone permissions are essential for the app to function. Without these permissions, you cannot participate in video calls.

Control: Revoke permissions anytime in Android Settings > Apps > Prof Dr Mahmoud Selim > Permissions

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

  • Meeting History: Stored locally until you clear it
  • Video & Audio: Not recorded (real-time only)
  • Call Data: Discarded immediately when call ends

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will display a prominent notice in the app when changes occur.
